Xia Sheng's Garden

A garden that endures through eternity, unchanged.
Each character dwells in her own corner,
cycling through her story without an end.

一座歷經永劫而不毀壞分毫的花園，
這些角色們各自據居角落，
在她們的故事中輪迴。
